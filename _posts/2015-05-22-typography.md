Typography definitions and resources.

## Definitions
- Typography: Arranging type to make writing readable and appealing.
  - Typefaces
  - Point size
  - Line length
  - Line-spacing (leading)
  - Letter-spacing (tracking)
  - Spacing within letter pairs (kerning)
